Principal's Message

(Anupa Pushpa Tirkey)

Welcome to S.S.+2 High School, Gola, where we nurture young minds from Class 1 to Class 12. I, Anupa Pushpa Tirkey, am honored to be the principal of this esteemed institution.

As we strive for excellence in education, our mission is to provide a supportive and inclusive environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and character development. Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to helping each student reach their full potential.

From the foundational years of Class 1 to the threshold of higher education in Class 12, we aim to instill values of integrity, resilience, and compassion. We believe in empowering our students to become responsible citizens, innovative thinkers, and leaders of tomorrow.

I invite you to explore our website and discover the opportunities and experiences that S.S.+2 High School, Gola, has to offer. Together, let us shape the future of our children and make a positive impact on the world.

Thank you for entrusting us with the privilege of educating your child.

Sincerely,
Anupa Pushpa Tirkey
Principal, S.S.+2 High School, Gola
